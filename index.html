    Exploring the Abstract Factory Design Pattern in Swift

    The Abstract Factory is a creational design pattern that allows you to produce families of related objects without specifying their exact classes. It provides an interface for creating various types of objects, abstracting away the concrete implementations. This is particularly useful in scenarios where you need to create products that are closely related and compatible with a specific platform or context.
